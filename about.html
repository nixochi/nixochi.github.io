about

i spent years in academia. it taught me how to research, how to write, and how to wait. it also taught me how easy it is to look away. eventually, i left.

these days i work differently. i make tools and visualizations that try to make complicated things clearer. sometimes the focus is science, sometimes it's justice. the goal is the same: to make knowledge more shareable.

i take cues from music, art, and the neighborhoods around me. the details of a wall, a flyer, a conversation — they matter as much as any dataset. technology is only part of it.
